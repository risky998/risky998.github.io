Project: Ezrabox

An affordable storage platorm @ Cornell University

Ezrabox - An Overview

Students at Cornell are unable to hold their dorm rooms over summer. Many make long trips to come to Cornell in the first place and for some, such as international students, taking all their belongings back home is not a feasible option. Ezrabox aims to connect student renters with potential hosts, i.e students or residents who own or rent homes year-round in the Ithaca area.

My Work on Ezrabox

I personally worked on creating a functional backend with required methods and REST API implementation for this project. table. The backend was developed using Flask, a framework in Python. A PostgreSQL database was used for the project.

Convenient

Everything, from searching listings to bookings to payments is all handled on the website. Hosts indicate their willingness to provide transport.

Affordable

Ezrabox is far cheaper than other all other such solutions on Cornell's campus, allowing students to save money but have a reliable source of summer storage.
